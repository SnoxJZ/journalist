Our history

Our association/collegium of editors and journalists was established to unite professionals dedicated to high-quality analytics and research. From the very beginning, we were a small group of experts bound by a shared goal and commitment to excellence. Over time, we have grown into a recognized resource for businesses, becoming a reliable partner for companies. Today, our services are sought after by companies aiming to gain deeper insights into their markets and understand the competitive environment. Our international presence, with offices in the USA, Germany, and Japan, allows us to provide regionally adapted solutions. Global network offers clients valuable insights tailored to the specifics of their markets. We have extensive experience, which enables us to successfully manage even the most complex projects.

Our collegium operates at the intersection of journalism and analytical expertise, providing clients with reliable data and objective recommendations. Our research supports companies in making informed decisions and staying competitive.
