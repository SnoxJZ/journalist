Activities of our organization

Our association is an international community of professionals in journalism, analytics, and research with offices in the USA, Germany, and Japan. We work with businesses, providing comprehensive services such as market reviews, comparative analysis of products and services, and in-depth industry and company analytics. Our projects help clients better understand the competitive landscape and make well-informed strategic decisions.

Our team includes experienced journalists, researchers, and emerging specialists, whom we actively support through training seminars and offline events. With expertise in market research, financial, and technical analysis, we ensure the high quality and depth of our projects, making our association a reliable partner for businesses and the scientific community.
